Man o Salwa — Restaurant in Barcelona

Man - O - Salwa serves authentic, halal Pakistani and Indian cuisine in the heart of Barcelona.

Our menu features traditional dishes such as Goat Karahi, Kababs, Biryani, Naan, and Nihari, prepared with rich spices and authentic recipes.

Cuisine: Authentic Pakistani & Indian (Halal)
Specialties: Karahi, Kababs, Naan, Biryani, Nihari
Services: Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery & Catering

Man - O - Salwa has multiple locations worldwide, including a notable restaurant in Barcelona, as well as locations in Clayton, Australia and Pickering, Ontario.
